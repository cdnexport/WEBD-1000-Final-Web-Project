WebPaint begun it's life in mid-November 2017 as a browser extension for Firefox(GitHub for that can be found here), a side project to develop my development skills beyond what the college was teaching me.

When the due date for a final project for my web development course came closer WebPaint was opportunistically ported to a full web page format, and the site was built around it.
